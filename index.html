03 — The broken workflow

Three leaks.
Every load.

If you’re looking for “the app for car haulers,” it doesn’t exist yet—that’s the hole we’re filling. These failure modes repeat at scale because nothing sits in the loop with the driver.

01
The Yard
Loading from memory, every time
💸$150–$300 lost when a vehicle doesn't fit and is left behind

The driver receives a paper load sheet. No guidance on configuration, no weight check, no verification. When something goes wrong, vehicles get unloaded and reloaded. What should take 90 minutes takes half a day.

02
The Hunt
Navigating the yard without a route
⏱20–45 minutes lost per load walking the yard

Bay codes on the load sheet are positions scattered across a facility spanning several city blocks. Some yards have a basic diagram. Most have painted rows on asphalt. None give the driver an optimized pickup route for their specific load.

03
The Delivery
Arriving blind at the dealer
🗺15–30 minutes lost per stop at an unfamiliar dealer

The load sheet gives an address — not the entrance, the driveway, or how to position an 80-foot rig in a tight street. Experienced drivers know from memory. New drivers guess. Every wrong approach is income off the next run.

"

One bad stack. A reload. Another unit that still doesn’t fit. A two-hour run becomes a five-hour firefight—inside the same HOS clock. That isn’t anecdotal; it’s the default when software stops at dispatch.

04 — Product

Software
where the rig is.

MONTAR is a mobile execution layer: constraints-aware load planning, sequenced yard pickup, and dealer-curb guidance—the workflows TMS never instrumented. AI proposes; the driver decides. Evidence over black-box magic.

Before the first vehicle moves
The load is optimized and verified

MONTAR delivers an optimized load plan — verified against legal weight limits, physical rig constraints, and the driver's own preferences. Configuration conflicts are caught before anyone drives up the ramp. The driver confirms the plan, sees the reasoning, and can adjust with full visibility into the impact of any change.

In the yard
Every vehicle found in the right order

Bay codes become a live, sequenced pickup route across the yard — rail yard or port, single OEM or multi-brand. MONTAR maps the vehicle locations and guides the driver through the pickup in the exact sequence the load plan requires. No backtracking. No wasted steps. Load and go.

At the dealer door
Intelligently guided delivery — every time

MONTAR knows the dealer — the right entrance, the correct approach angle, and how to position an 80-foot rig without a wrong turn. Arriving without this information risks damage to the vehicle, the rig, or both. MONTAR eliminates that risk with guided delivery intelligence at every drop-off.

MONTAR suggests.
Driver decides.

Explainable plans—not black boxes. Preferences and on-the-ground judgment fold into constraints. If someone overrides, they see breakage before the ramp, not at the dealer. That’s how you get adoption in a liability-heavy job.

Distribution reality

Drivers switch carriers and formats constantly. MONTAR normalizes fragmented load sheets so the product sticks to the driver—not to a single TMS rollout.
